About

THE PROJECT

The client had an application for self improvement which they wanted to revamp and reached out to us to help them with their idea. We were asked to join a cool team in the United States, with a great idea and progress in its implementation. The main challenges were to create a website for administrators to manage the application and create content dynamically, that could be integrated and work seamlessly with the iOS app. We had to implement services to feed the native iOS app and admin sections. We integrated a decision motor to the project. We also built an environment for test, demos and production. We performed automatization of tasks and integrated tools of continuous integration to the project.

About

THE PROCESS

SOLUTION

We worked on a per Sprint basis, completing different tasks in the back-end such as: configuring test, demo and production environments using DigitalOcean, creating backend modules adding authorization, authentication, integration with Activity, and CRUD methods for several entities. We also have been working on a responsive front-end based on Angular and a native iOS application. We communicated with the US Team daily, we followed through the whole process with SCRUM, and used Jira and Slack as a means of communication. We performed continuous development of new functionalities and added test to all of our services.

RESULTS

We managed to continuously deliver new features, adding tests for all our services, implementing services to feed native iOS app & administration section, integrating decision engine project as well as performing task automation and adding continuous integration tools.
